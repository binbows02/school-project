UX / UI Designing

The problem

At first glance, the website looks structured. But whn you look deeper, theres a lot of flaws when it comes to user experience and design.

I and my friend's task were to:

  • Make a interactive styleguide
  • Study and apply design patterns to our redesign
  • Design a mobile home page screen

Styleguide

We've decided to work on the styleguide first. This way when we disagree on something, we dont have to change everything within the website itself.

This will save us a lot of time in the future.

Redesigning in Figma

Once that's finished, we immediately work on our hi-fi prototype, because we want to visualize the final look and feel of the website as soon as possible. This helps us spot any design inconsistencies early and allows us to gather feedback from our classmates.

Another factor comes from the fact that we needed the hi-fi prototype to apply our design patterns.

By working step by step, it helped us stay organized and allowed us to redesign with the design patterns used.

Let's make something together! :)
